How To Play

The object of to game is the be the first player to the winning total.


Players can determine the winning total by entering a number in the "Winning Score" field and clicking on the "Winning Score" button.

The default Winning Score is 100.


Click the "Roll Dice" button to roll the dice.

The player can roll the dice as many times as they like.

When the player has a score they are happy with they can click the "Hold" button to "bank" their score.


When a player "holds" their score they forfeit their turn.


If a player rolls a 1 they forfeit their turn.


If a player rolls two 6's in a row they forfeit their turn AND lose all of their points.


To play again, click "New Game".



HAVE FUN!
